## 📖 About The Project

**Terminal Casino** brings the thrill of Las Vegas directly to your command line. Built entirely in core Python, this lightweight and fast text-based application lets you test your luck and manage your bankroll across classic casino games—no external dependencies or GUI required!

## ✨ Features

- **🍒 Slot Machine**: Spin the emoji-based reels! Experience animated spins, matching bonuses, and massive jackpot payouts.
- **🎡 Roulette Table**: The classic wheel experience! Bet on exact numbers for high-risk payouts (`35:1`) or play it safe with Even/Odd wagers (`1:1`).
- **💰 Bankroll Management**: Start with a fixed balance, track your winnings reliably, and try not to go broke before you cash out.
- **⌨️ Intuitive CLI**: Easy-to-read prompts, structured outputs, and smooth time-delayed animations for maximum suspense.

## 🚀 Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to get your local casino running in seconds.

### Prerequisites

This project uses standard libraries. All you need is **Python 3.6** or higher installed on your system.

### Installation & Execution

1. **Clone the repository**
   ```sh
   git clone https://github.com/buildwithnahin/casino-games.git
   ```
2. **Navigate to the directory**
   ```sh
   cd casino-games
   ```
3. **Run the game**
   ```sh
   python3 main.py
   ```

## 🎮 How to Play

Upon launching, you will be given a starting balance of `$1000`. You can choose from the main menu:

1. **Play Slots**: Enter your bet. 3 matching symbols win `10x` your bet, 2 matches win `2x`.
2. **Play Roulette**: Enter your bet. Guess the exact number to win `35x` your bet, or guess Even/Odd to double it.
3. **Cash Out**: Leave the casino with your current bankroll.

*But beware... The House always has the edge!*
